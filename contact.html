Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our tiffin service

What are your delivery timings?

We deliver meals at the following times:

  • Breakfast: 7:00 AM - 9:00 AM
  • Lunch: 12:00 PM - 2:00 PM
  • Dinner: 7:00 PM - 9:00 PM

You can specify your preferred time slot when placing an order.

How do I place an order?

You can place an order through:

  • Our website's order form
  • Phone call: +91 98765 43210
  • WhatsApp: +91 98765 43210
  • Email: info@hometaste.com

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept the following payment methods:

  • Cash on Delivery
  • UPI (Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm)
  • Bank Transfer
  • Credit/Debit Cards

Can I customize my meals?

Yes! We offer customization options including:

  • Spice level preferences (mild, medium, spicy)
  • Dietary restrictions (vegetarian, non-vegetarian)
  • Food allergies accommodation
  • Special requests for weekly/monthly plans

What if I'm not satisfied with the food?

Customer satisfaction is our priority. If you're not happy with your meal:

  • Contact us immediately at +91 98765 43210
  • We'll investigate the issue promptly
  • Replacement meal or refund will be provided
  • We continuously improve based on feedback

How far in advance should I place an order?

Order timing recommendations:

  • Daily orders: At least 2 hours in advance
  • Weekly plans: 1 day in advance
  • Monthly plans: 2-3 days in advance
  • Bulk orders: 1 week in advance
